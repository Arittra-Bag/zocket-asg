fun:
- Use emojis
- Add playful, humorous language
- Keep it short and snappy
- Use exclamations and puns

professional:
- Formal tone, no emojis
- Focus on value and benefits
- Clarity > Creativity
- Use stats or direct CTAs

semi-fun:
- Conversational tone with mild formality
- One emoji per message max
- Blend enthusiasm with structure

Meta:
- Visual + emoji-friendly
- Works well with hashtags
- CTA buttons common

Google:
- Concise headline and description
- Strict character limits
- No emojis or slang
- Must contain high-ranking keywords

LinkedIn:
- Clear, professional narrative
- No emojis
- Emphasize business value
- Avoid aggressive CTAs
